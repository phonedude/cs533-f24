Carter Beauford is my favorite drummer.

Drummers are musicians and Carter is my favorite because of how musical he is. That said, Carter is a "drummer's" drummer. He is a master of his craft and has an open style of playing that's unmatchable (left-hand lead on a right-hand kit)--the result of watching Buddy Rich on TV at such a young age (two years old) he didn't realize, when he set the drums up to mirror Buddy's kit, he was actually training himself to play left-handed.

Because of this simple "mistake," Carter began a journey of drumming with less restriction -- his unique style affording an ambidextrous ability to easily move around the drums and fludily play beats no matter which hand played which drum.

Oddly almost a footnote in his storied musical career (playing with the likes of legends such as Victor Wooten and Bela Fleck), for those who aren't aware, he is the long-time drummer for the Dave Matthews Band.
